# 🏎️ Porsche — A Really Cool Car Website

Hey there! This is a super cool website about **Porsche** — one of the fastest and fanciest cars in the whole world! 🚗💨

## What Is This?

It's a website that shows you everything about Porsche cars. When you open it, you see a big picture of a beautiful car and a message that says **"Success is a derivative of defiance"** — that's a fancy way of saying **"You win by being brave and different!"**

## What Can You See?

The website has **5 fun parts** you can scroll through:

### 🏁 1. The Big Welcome (Hero)
A giant, dark, movie-like picture of a Porsche car. It looks SO cool, like it's from a superhero movie! There's a button that says **"Enter the Atelier"** — that means "come inside the workshop."

### 📅 2. The Car Timeline (History)
You can scroll sideways and see how Porsche cars changed over the years:
- **1948** — The very first Porsche was born! It had only 40 horsepower (that's like 40 horses pulling it 🐴)
- **1963** — The famous **911** was made. It looks the same even today!
- **1974** — They added a **turbo** — like a rocket boost for the engine! 🚀
- **2026** — The newest one! It uses electricity AND fuel together. Super smart! ⚡

### ⚙️ 3. How The Car Works (Engineering)
This part shows you the car's insides — like looking inside a toy to see how it works:
- **The Engine** — It has 6 cylinders laid flat (like pancakes 🥞)
- **The Gearbox** — It changes gears faster than you can blink! Under 100 milliseconds!
- **The Suspension** — It reads the road 1,000 times every second so you don't feel bumps

### 🏆 4. All The Wins (Victories)
Porsche has won **19 times** at a very long race called **Le Mans** (it goes on for 24 hours straight!). They also hold records at a really twisty track called **Nürburgring** with 73 turns! 😮

### 🎨 5. Make Your Own (Sonderwunsch)
"Sonderwunsch" means **"special wish"** in German. You can pick your own:
- Leather colors and stitching
- Carbon fiber parts (super strong and light material)
- Basically anything you want! It's like building your dream car with LEGOs, but real! 🧱

## What Does It Look Like?

- **Colors**: Super dark black (like nighttime 🌑), creamy white, and a little bit of red ❤️
- **Fonts**: Clean and modern letters that look fancy
- **Pictures**: Close-up shots of leather stitching and shiny carbon fiber — you can almost touch them!

## Cool Stuff It Does

✨ Things pop up as you scroll down (like magic!)
✨ The car parts "fly in" and build themselves on screen
✨ Numbers count up really fast (like a stopwatch!)
✨ Buttons feel like real switches when you press them
✨ The timeline you can drag with your mouse

## How To Open It

1. Download all the files
2. Open your terminal (the black screen with typing)
3. Type this:
```
npx serve ./
```
4. Open your web browser and go to **http://localhost:3000**
5. That's it! Enjoy the cars! 🎉

## What's Inside The Folder?


## Built With

- **HTML** — The bones of the website (structure)
- **CSS** — The clothes of the website (how it looks)
- **JavaScript** — The brain of the website (how it moves and thinks)
- No extra libraries — it's all handmade! 🤲
